This page is an interactive viewer for my driver harmonic distortion measurements, so you can compare how different drivers behave under Voltage Drive (VD) and Current Drive (CD).
The motivation is simple: instead of comparing one static plot at a time, you can overlay multiple drivers and directly compare distortion plots.

How to use it: pick one harmonic (H2, H3, or H5), then select any combination of drivers and VD/CD checkboxes in the left panel. The legend below lists the active traces. Use search and the bulk-select buttons to build comparisons quickly.
